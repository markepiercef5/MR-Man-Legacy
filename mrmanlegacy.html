Meet the Men Behind MR Man Legacy

Led by Mark Pierce & Rick Krug

MR Man Legacy is built on the conviction that men grow best when they are challenged well, listened to seriously, and surrounded by honest brotherhood. Mark and Rick bring that conviction to every event, group, and conversation.

Mark Pierce

Licensed Professional Counselor · Men's Guide

Mark Pierce, known as “Homie” and now “GPops” to the men and family he loves, is the founder of MR Man Legacy and a Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) with a master’s degree in Clinical Mental Health Counseling. Born in Iowa and a lifelong Iowa Hawkeyes fan, Mark brings the same passion and loyalty to the men he serves. He has been married to his wife for 21 years, is a proud father of three, and recently stepped into the joy of grandfatherhood with his new grandson. As both an entrepreneur and a mental health professional, Mark blends clinical insight with real-world experience to help men build stronger character, deeper relationships, and a clear sense of purpose in every area of life.

Rick Krug

Coach · Speaker · Ministry Leader

Rick has spent over three decades doing one thing in dozens of different rooms: helping people tell the truth about where they actually are. He's a Certified Business Coach with a master's in theology from Pepperdine, decades of pastoral and teaching experience, and — somewhat unexpectedly — a background in stand-up comedy. That last one matters more than it sounds. It means Rick knows how to read a room, sit in a silence, and tell a story that lands. He's spent his career in the space where biblical truth meets real life — teaching, preaching, coaching, and writing for men who are tired of performing and ready for something honest.
